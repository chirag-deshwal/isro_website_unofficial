2013

MANGALYAAN

India becomes the first nation to reach Mars orbit on its maiden attempt. The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) famously cost less than the Hollywood movie 'Gravity', showcasing ISRO's frugal engineering excellence.

2019

CHANDRAYAAN-2

A complex mission featuring an Orbiter, Lander (Vikram), and Rover (Pragyan). While the soft landing wasn't successful, the Orbiter continues to provide invaluable scientific data from lunar orbit.

2023

ADITYA-L1

India's first solar observatory mission. Positioned at the Lagrange Point 1 (L1), approximately 1.5 million km from Earth, to study the Sun's corona, solar winds, and space weather phenomena continuously.
