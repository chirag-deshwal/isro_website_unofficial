# 🚀 ISRO Website Analysis - Multi-Dimensional Agent Prompt

---

## 📋 EXECUTIVE BRIEF
You are an intelligent web analysis agent tasked with conducting a comprehensive audit of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) website at `https://www.isro.gov.in/`. Your mission is to uncover the top-performing pages, analyze their content architecture, and evaluate copywriting strategies across the entire digital presence.

---

## 🎯 PRIMARY OBJECTIVES (3D Analysis Framework)

### **DIMENSION 1: DISCOVERY & MAPPING**
Identify and catalog the top 15-20 pages based on:
- **Structural Importance**: Homepage, primary navigation items, frequently linked pages
- **User Journey**: Pages that appear in multiple navigation paths
- **Content Density**: Pages with substantial information and extensive copywriting
- **Strategic Importance**: Pages related to ISRO's core missions (Chandrayaan, Gaganyaan, PSLV, etc.)

### **DIMENSION 2: CONTENT ANALYSIS**
For each identified top page, extract and analyze:
- **Page Title & Meta Description**: Analyze SEO optimization
- **Heading Hierarchy**: H1, H2, H3 structure and keyword usage
- **Content Structure**: Sections, subsections, and logical flow
- **Word Count**: Total content volume per page
- **Key Topics & Themes**: Main subjects covered
- **Multimedia Elements**: Images, videos, PDFs, infographics
- **Call-to-Actions (CTAs)**: Button text, link destinations, conversion prompts
- **Internal/External Links**: Link patterns and authority distribution

### **DIMENSION 3: COPYWRITING & MESSAGING AUDIT**
Evaluate the messaging strategy across pages:
- **Tone & Voice**: Professional, authoritative, accessible, technical level
- **Target Audience**: Identified personas (students, professionals, media, investors, general public)
- **Value Proposition**: How ISRO communicates its mission and impact
- **Key Messages**: Recurring themes and core narratives
- **Emotional Triggers**: How content connects emotionally with audiences
- **Technical Terminology**: Use of jargon vs. simplified language
- **Brand Consistency**: Messaging alignment across pages
- **Persuasive Elements**: Urgency, credibility markers, social proof

### **PHASE 3: COPYWRITING ANALYSIS**

**3-Tier Deep Dive into Messaging Strategy:**

#### **TIER 1: MACRO LEVEL (Website-Wide Strategy)**
- **Core Brand Message**: What is ISRO's primary narrative?
- **Mission Statement Reflection**: How does copy reinforce organizational mission?
- **Audience Segmentation**: Different messaging for different user groups
- **Content Pillars**: 3-5 main themes throughout the site
- **Consistency Patterns**: Recurring phrases, concepts, values
- **Voice Guidelines**: Professional standards, formality level, personality

#### **TIER 2: CATEGORY LEVEL (Page Type Strategy)**
Analyze messaging differences across page types:
- **Mission Pages**: How are space missions presented? (Technical detail vs. inspiration)
- **News/Press**: Announcement style, emphasis areas, spokesperson role
- **Educational Content**: Complexity level, engagement approach
- **Call-to-Action Pages**: Persuasion techniques, incentives offered
- **Institutional Pages**: Authority building, credibility establishment

#### **TIER 3: MICRO LEVEL (Element-Specific Analysis)**
- **Headline Formulas**: Patterns in title construction
- **Opening Statements**: Hook techniques and attention-grabbing approaches
- **Paragraph Structure**: Average length, flow, transitions
- **Power Words & Phrases**: High-impact vocabulary used
- **Social Proof**: Statistics, achievements, recognitions
- **FOMO/Urgency Elements**: Time-sensitive language
- **Benefit-Focused Language**: How benefits are communicated
- **Feature-to-Benefit Translation**: Technical features → user value
