CHANDRAYAAN-3

CONQUERING THE SOUTH POLE

India makes history as the first nation to successfully soft-land on the lunar South Pole, demonstrating end-to-end capability in safe landing and roving on the lunar surface.

THE JOURNEY

PHASE 01

EARTH CENTRIC

Launch by LVM3-M4, followed by a series of Earth bound maneuvers to raise the orbit height before translunar injection.

PHASE 02

LUNAR TRANSFER

Trajectory transfer to Moon's sphere of influence, followed by Lunar Orbit Insertion and orbit reduction maneuvers.

PHASE 03

SOFT LANDING

Power descent phase initiated from 30km altitude, performing rough braking and fine braking to achieve a precise soft landing near the South Pole.
