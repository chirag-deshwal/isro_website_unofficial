ISRO CENTRES

THE BUILDING BLOCKS OF INNOVATION

ISRO spans across the nation with specialized facilities dedicated to launch capabilities, satellite building, tracking, applications, and research.

Home / About / Centres

MAJOR ISRO CENTRES

VSSC

THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre

The lead centre for the development of launch vehicle technology. Focuses on aeronautics, avionics, materials, and comprehensive launch vehicle architecture.

SPECIALIZATION: Launch Vehicles

URSC

BENGALURU

U R Rao Satellite Centre

The lead centre for building Indian satellites. Involved in design, development, and realization of communication, navigation, and scientific satellites.

SPECIALIZATION: Satellites

SDSC

SRIHARIKOTA

Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SHAR)

The Spaceport of India. It provides launch base infrastructure for Indian space programmes and processes solid propellant boosters.

SPECIALIZATION: Launch Base & Infrastructure

LPSC

VALIAMALA

Liquid Propulsion Systems Centre

Pioneers in liquid propulsion for ISRO's Launch Vehicles and Spacecrafts. Develops Earth-storable and cryogenic propulsion systems.

SPECIALIZATION: Propulsion

IIRS

DEHRADUN

Indian Institute of Remote Sensing

A premier training and educational institute for capacity building in Remote Sensing, Geoinformatics, and GPS technology.

SPECIALIZATION: Training & Education

NRSC

HYDERABAD

National Remote Sensing Centre

Responsible for satellite data acquisition, processing, dissemination, and remote sensing applications like early warning for disasters.

SPECIALIZATION: Remote Sensing Data

SAC

AHMEDABAD

Space Applications Centre

Focuses on the design of space-borne instruments and development of space applications including communications and remote sensing.

SPECIALIZATION: Space Applications

ISTRAC

BENGALURU

ISRO Telemetry, Tracking and Command Network

Provides tracking support for all ISRO satellites and launch vehicle missions. Plays a key role during the critical phases of missions.

SPECIALIZATION: Tracking & Command
