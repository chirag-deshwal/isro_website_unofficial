MISSION OVERVIEW

The Gaganyaan project envisages demonstration of human spaceflight capability by launching a crew of 3 members to an orbit of 400 km for a 3 days mission and bring them back safely to earth, by landing in Indian sea waters.

HUMAN SPACE FLIGHT CENTRE

The primary mandate of HSFC is to spearhead ISRO's Gaganyaan programme through coordinated efforts and focus all activities carried out in other ISRO centres, research labs in India, Indian academia and Industries towards accomplishing the mission.

HSFC conforms to high standards of reliability and human safety in undertaking R&D activities in new technology areas, such as:
